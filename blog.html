AN UPDATE ON WORK IN THE WEB

Every year we host Work In The Web, a free 3 day workshop for students who want to work in the web industry. We cover skills and principles which are key to becoming a web professional, helping students get a head start in our ever changing industry. THE SUCCESS OF 2020 Back in January, we had 20 students from across the country join us for 3 days of hands-on workshops, guest speakers and exciting social events. We covered a wide range of development skills and discussed in depth some of the best ways to start out a career in the web field. We were humbled by the overwhelmingly positive response from our attendees. It motivated us to keep progressing the event further and continue giving back to the people who make this industry great.

Rose Jane
